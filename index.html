Molasses Rescue Strategy

A turn-based strategy game about the Great Molasses Flood of 1919

Turn: 1
Actions: 5 / 5
Rescued: 0 / 5
Caught by flood: 0

Mission

Use your action points to move, rescue people, and build barriers before the molasses spreads.

Strategy Log

How to Play

  • Move: click a nearby square. Cost: 1 action.
  • Rescue: stand on or next to a person. Cost: 1 action.
  • Build Barrier: click a nearby empty square. Cost: 2 actions.
  • End Turn: molasses spreads after your turn.
  • Win: rescue everyone you can, then return to the green safe zone.

Strategy Tips

  • Do not spend every turn running. Sometimes a barrier buys more time.
  • Rescue people near the left side early because the flood starts there.
  • Use buildings as natural blockers and place barriers in narrow paths.
  • The molasses spreads faster as turns pass, so plan routes before moving.
